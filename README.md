# El-Daniel Keypad
**Precision and Clarity in Every Swipe.**

El-Daniel Keypad is an Android IME (Input Method Editor) that replaces the clutter of 30+ tiny buttons with **8 clear directional swipe paths**. Inspired by the clarity and excellence of Daniel, it is built for seniors, people with limited dexterity, high-speed typists, and anyone seeking a more intentional and fluid way to communicate.

### ✨ Key Features
- **Swift Daniel Logic**: Vowels (↑), Power Consonants (→), and Flow Consonants (↓) are just a swipe away.
- **Aggressive Swipe-to-Delete**: Long swipe left instantly clears your text — no more hunting for backspace.
- **Two-Finger Tapdown Pivot**: Anchor direction with one finger and execute with the second across a full 360° space.
- **Send & Return**: Dedicated buttons for instant messaging and line breaks.

### 📲 Mobile Download & Installation (No PC Required)

If you are on an Android device and want to test the keypad layout immediately without building from source, you can install our pre-compiled package directly:

1. **[👉 Download El-Daniel Keypad APK (Latest Beta Version)](https://github.com/launchbypatrickwebdev-creator/El-Daniel-Easy-Stroke-Keypad/releases/download/v1.0.0/El-Daniel-Keypad-v1.0-Beta.apk)**

2. **Allow Installation**  
   Your device may show a security warning. Go to:  
   **Settings > Apps > Special App Access > Install Unknown Apps** → Enable for your Browser or Files app.

3. **Activate the Keypad**  
   Go to **Settings → Languages & Input → On-screen Keyboard / Manage Keyboards** and toggle **ON** for **El-Daniel Keypad**.

### The Final "El Daniel" Blueprint

| Zone                  | Direction       | Letters / Symbols                  | Count |
|-----------------------|-----------------|------------------------------------|-------|
| **Vowels**            | ↑ Up            | A E I O U Y                        | 6     |
| **Power Consonants**  | → Right         | T N S R H D                        | 6     |
| **Flow Consonants**   | ↓ Down          | L C M F P B                        | 6     |
| **Complex Consonants**| ← Left          | W G V K X Q J Z                    | 8     |
| **Numbers 1-5**       | ↗ Up-Right      | 1 2 3 4 5                          | 5     |
| **Numbers 6-0**       | ↘ Down-Right    | 6 7 8 9 0                          | 5     |
| **Punctuation**       | ↖ Up-Left       | . , ? ! '                          | 5     |
| **Symbols**           | ↙ Down-Left     | @ # & / - + × ÷ = etc.             | -     |
